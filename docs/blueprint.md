# **App Name**: SnapTrack

## Core Features:

- Snap and Detect: Users can snap a photo of their meal, and the app will use AI to detect the food items present, using an AI tool.
- Calorie Estimation: The app estimates the calorie count and nutritional information for the detected food items.
- SnapYourPlate Challenge: A social challenge feature where users can upload photos of their meals and compete with others.
- Founder Story: A section on the landing page to tell the story of the app's creation and mission.
- Blog Integration: Integrate a blog to provide nutrition insights, recipe ideas, and challenge updates.
- User Testimonials: Display user testimonials with photos and locations to build trust and credibility.
- Global Leaderboard: A leaderboard displaying the top food snaps and users, encouraging community engagement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest Green (#228B22) to convey health and natural vibes.
- Background color: Light Mint (#F0FFF0) for a fresh and clean feel.
- Accent color: Sunny Orange (#FFA500) to highlight key actions and calls to action.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif for headlines.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif for longer text.
- Use clean, modern icons related to food, health, and social sharing.
- Subtle animations for the 'SnapYourPlate' gallery carousel to keep the page engaging.
